43 
 * <p>
44 
 * {@code allowedAbbreviationLength} specifies how many consecutive capital letters are
45 
 * allowed in the identifier.
46 
 * A value of <i>3</i> indicates that up to 4 consecutive capital letters are allowed,
47

48

49

50

51

57

59
